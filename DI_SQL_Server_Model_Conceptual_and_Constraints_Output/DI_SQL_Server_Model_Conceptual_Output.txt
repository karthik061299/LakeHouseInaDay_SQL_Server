Description:   Conceptual Data Model for Workforce Management and Consulting Business Reporting System
------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. DOMAIN OVERVIEW
========================================================================

The conceptual data model represents a comprehensive Workforce Management and Consulting Business domain. This system manages the complete lifecycle of consulting engagements including:

• Employee/Consultant Management - Tracking workforce information, skills, and employment status
• Project and Client Management - Managing client relationships, projects, and engagements
• Time and Attendance Tracking - Recording billable and non-billable hours
• Hiring and Onboarding Processes - Managing recruitment workflows and candidate information
• Financial Management - Tracking billing rates, costs, margins, and revenue
• Resource Allocation - Assigning consultants to projects and managing utilization

The primary business focus is on managing consulting resources, tracking their assignments to client projects, monitoring time worked, and calculating financial metrics such as gross profit and margins.


2. LIST OF ENTITIES WITH DESCRIPTIONS
========================================================================

1. Employee Resource
   Description: Represents individual consultants, contractors, and employees working on various projects. This is the core entity tracking workforce information including personal details, employment status, skills, and assignment history.

2. Client Organization
   Description: Represents client companies and organizations that engage consulting services. Includes both direct clients and end clients, tracking organizational details, sectors, and relationship information.

3. Project Engagement
   Description: Represents specific consulting projects or engagements where resources are assigned. Tracks project details, locations, duration, billing arrangements, and project categorization.

4. Time Entry
   Description: Captures daily time worked by consultants including regular hours, overtime, double time, and various leave types. Forms the basis for billing and payroll calculations.

5. Hiring Workflow
   Description: Manages the end-to-end hiring and onboarding process for new consultants including workflow stages, approvals, and process tracking.

6. Project Assignment
   Description: Links employees to specific projects with associated rates, dates, and assignment details. Represents the relationship between resources and their project allocations.

7. Recruitment Request
   Description: Represents job requisitions and hiring needs for specific positions, including requirements, skills needed, and request details.

8. Financial Rate Structure
   Description: Defines billing rates, pay rates, and associated financial terms for project assignments including standard time, overtime, and various rate components.

9. Organizational Hierarchy
   Description: Represents the internal organizational structure including business units, departments, regions, and reporting relationships.

10. Client Agreement
    Description: Captures contractual terms and conditions between the organization and clients including payment terms, invoicing arrangements, and agreement details.

11. Working Calendar
    Description: Defines working days, holidays, and business day calculations for different locations and regions to support accurate time and billing calculations.

12. Skill Profile
    Description: Represents technical and professional skills associated with employees and project requirements for matching and resource planning.

4. KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs)
========================================================================

1. Gross Profit (GP)
   Description: Total gross profit generated from consulting engagements, calculated as revenue minus direct costs
   Formula: Revenue - Direct Costs

2. Gross Profit Margin (GPM)
   Description: Gross profit as a percentage of revenue, indicating profitability of engagements
   Formula: (Gross Profit / Revenue) × 100

3. Net Bill Rate (NBR)
   Description: Net billing rate charged to clients after adjustments and discounts

4. Markup Percentage
   Description: Percentage markup applied on pay rate to determine bill rate
   Formula: ((Bill Rate - Pay Rate) / Pay Rate) × 100

5. Actual Markup
   Description: Actual markup percentage achieved on assignments

6. Headcount Metrics:
   - Begin HC: Headcount at the beginning of the period
   - End HC: Headcount at the end of the period
   - Starts - New Project: New hires starting on projects
   - Starts - Internal Movements: Internal transfers and movements
   - Terms: Terminations during the period
   - Other Project Ends: Project completions
   - OffBoard: Offboarding completions
   - Net Addition: Net change in headcount

7. Voluntary Terminations
   Description: Number of voluntary terminations during the period

8. Derived Revenue
   Description: Calculated revenue based on hours worked and billing rates

9. Derived Gross Profit
   Description: Calculated gross profit from derived revenue

10. Backlog Revenue
    Description: Revenue in backlog not yet recognized

11. Backlog Gross Profit
    Description: Gross profit associated with backlog revenue

12. Expected Hours
    Description: Expected billable hours for the period

13. Expected Total Hours
    Description: Total expected hours including all categories

14. Utilization Rate
    Description: Percentage of available hours that are billable
    Formula: (Billable Hours / Total Available Hours) × 100

15. Response Time
    Description: Time taken to respond to requisitions or candidates

16. PO End Count
    Description: Count of purchase orders ending in the period

17. Consultant Ageing
    Description: Number of days consultant has been on assignment

18. ACA Cost
    Description: Affordable Care Act related costs per employee

19. Billing Placement Net Fee
    Description: Net fee for placement billing

20. Submitted Bill Rate
    Description: Bill rate submitted in proposals

21. Client Interview Required Count
    Description: Number of positions requiring client interviews

22. Redeployment Hire Count
    Description: Number of internal redeployment hires

23. Hotbook Hire Count
    Description: Number of rapid/hotbook hires

24. Referral Fee Amount
    Description: Total referral fees paid

25. Time to Fill
    Description: Average time to fill open positions

26. Submission to Hire Ratio
    Description: Ratio of submissions to actual hires
